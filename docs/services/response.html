Services / Response

Response.

Bundled armed and medical response, dispatched on a clinical decision — not a guess. Nationwide, every province, 24/7.

Clinical Trigger

When a ListenUp conversation flags real and present danger, our clinical team triggers an armed and medical response unit to the caller's location. The decision is clinical. The dispatch is national. The liability stays where it belongs.

Armed Response Network

We partner with established armed response operators in all nine provinces. Dispatch is rapid, vetted and SLA-backed — every minute is logged and every outcome is reviewed.

Medical Co-Response

Where the case requires it, we co-dispatch a medical response unit alongside armed response. Common scenarios: suicide attempts, gender-based violence with injury, severe self-harm in progress.

Clinical Aftercare

Response is the start, not the end. Every dispatch is followed by a clinical handover — the person is met by a case manager that night and again the next morning. We don't disappear after the unit leaves the driveway.

This service is delivered by qualified clinicians and case managers across all nine provinces. To talk to someone about whether it's the right fit, message ListenUp — a real human, within minutes.
