Services / Mediation

Mediation.

Family, workplace and commercial disputes — resolved with a qualified neutral third party, not a courtroom.

Divorce & Family Mediation

A time- and cost-effective legal alternative whereby parties to a dispute can appoint a qualified neutral third party to act as mediator and facilitate an agreed settlement — divorce, maintenance, parenting, elder, education or family dispute. The mediator clarifies issues and explores areas of compromise so the dispute resolves without battling it out in court.

Workplace Mediation

An alternative, voluntary legal process companies use to resolve labour conflict before it escalates. Workplace disputes and negotiations at every level can be formally mediated by an experienced mediator and stay within the labour process — 'end the problem, not the relationship'.

Commercial Mediation

In South Africa there is substantial scope for commercial mediation as a key resolution mechanism for business disputes. The mediation process offers parties the chance to resolve disputes while preserving the business relationship — and saving the legal costs of litigation.

This service is delivered by qualified clinicians and case managers across all nine provinces. To talk to someone about whether it's the right fit, message ListenUp — a real human, within minutes.
