Services / Mediation

Mediation.

Family, workplace and commercial conflict — resolved in a room, not a courtroom.

Divorce & Family Mediation

Divorce, maintenance, parenting, elder care, family business. Most of these never need a courtroom — they need a calm room, a trained neutral person, and the time to actually hear each other. We help you find what you can agree on, where you can compromise, and how to walk out without setting fire to the relationships that still matter.

Workplace Mediation

When a workplace conflict starts pulling teams apart — between colleagues, between management and labour, between shareholders — we step in before the CCMA does. Voluntary, confidential, contained inside your business. The goal isn't to win the fight. It's to end the problem, not the relationship.

Commercial Mediation

Two businesses, one dispute, and a court process that will cost both sides more than the disagreement is worth. Mediation gets you in a room with a neutral expert, finds the version of the answer you can both live with, and gets you back to running the business. Most cases close in days, not years.

This service is delivered by qualified clinicians and case managers across all nine provinces. To talk to someone about whether it's the right fit, message ListenUp — a real human, within minutes.
