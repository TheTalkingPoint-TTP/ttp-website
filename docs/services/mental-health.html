Services / Mental Health

Mental Health.

Counselling, trauma response and family support — clinically supervised, real humans at the other end.

Wellness Assist

Individuals are given access to a qualified therapist to help address any problems or needs associated with their emotional health, wellbeing, relationships and productivity. This solution helps address and manage the high rate of mental illness recorded — so instead of struggling with stress, depression, anxiety, suicide or PTSD on your own, you talk to us.

Parenting Assist

We facilitate all the advice and support most parents need through our professional network — a registered therapist, social worker or counsellor in real time. This service empowers parents to understand and facilitate their children's behavioural, developmental and learning issues. We are here for you every step of the way.

Trauma Assist

A direct line to talk about and work through traumatic experiences — events that often involve a threat to life or safety — by debriefing them first. For anyone exposed to acute trauma, we provide holistic and practical 4-step support to return to daily life as soon as possible.

Teacher Assist

Necessary assistance to support teachers as they create an effective and positive learning environment. Alongside a comprehensive service, teachers get access to a qualified professional to help address problems or needs associated with their health, wellbeing, relationships and productivity.

Learner & Parent Assist

We work to create an effective learning environment that supports learners beyond the formal delivery of education. Our assistance programmes give learners and their parents the tools they need to reach their real potential.

This service is delivered by qualified clinicians and case managers across all nine provinces. To talk to someone about whether it's the right fit, message ListenUp — a real human, within minutes.
