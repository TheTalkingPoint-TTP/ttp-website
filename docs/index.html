A short check-in / 04

Not sure if you should reach out?

Five questions. Two minutes. We won't store anything — your answers don't leave your browser. We just want to help you decide what to do next.

Question 01

In the past two weeks, has it been hard to find joy in things you usually enjoy?

Question 02

Have you felt down, hopeless, or like there's a weight you can't shake?

Question 03

Has your sleep changed — too little, too much, or restless?

Question 04 — safety

Have you had thoughts about hurting yourself, or that the world might be better off without you?

Question 05

Have you been able to talk to anyone about how you're feeling?

What happens when you reach out / 06

No forms. No queue.
A real human picks up.

You don't have to explain everything up front. You don't have to be ready. You just have to send one message.

Step 01

First contact.

WhatsApp, call, or message. No forms. No questionnaires. The door is always open — 3am, Sunday, Christmas Day.

ListenUp answers.

A real, qualified human picks up — case manager, counsellor, or social worker. End-to-end encrypted. In your language. Usually within minutes.
